<a id="day-1-sapporo"></a>

# Day 1 -- Sapporo

## Morning

- 09:10 Arrive at New Chitose Airport
- 09:45 JR Rapid Airport train → Sapporo Station (approx. 40 min)
- 10:30 Drop luggage at hotel

## Afternoon

- 11:30 Lunch at Sapporo Ramen Yokocho (miso ramen)
- 13:00 Odori Park & Sapporo TV Tower
- 15:00 Former Hokkaido Government Office (Red Brick)

## Evening

- 17:30 Tanukikoji Shopping Street
- 19:00 Dinner at Sapporo Beer Garden (Genghis Khan)
- 21:00 Return to hotel

---

<a id="day-2-otaru"></a>

# Day 2 -- Otaru

## Morning

- 08:30 Breakfast at hotel
- 09:30 JR train Sapporo → Otaru (approx. 30 min)
- 10:15 Otaru Canal walk & photo spot

## Afternoon

- 11:30 Sushi lunch at Otaru Sankaku Market
- 13:00 Music Box Museum (Orgel-do)
- 14:30 LeTAO double fromage cheesecake & tea
- 15:30 Kitaichi Glass (browse glassware)

## Evening

- 17:00 JR train Otaru → Sapporo
- 18:00 Nijo Market for fresh seafood dinner
- 20:00 Pack and prepare for departure
