# Troubleshooting

## Common Issues

### Watch Won't Turn On

1. Charge the watch for at least 30 minutes
2. Press and hold the side button for 10 seconds
3. If the problem persists, contact support

### Screen Is Unresponsive

1. Force restart: hold both buttons for 15 seconds
2. Check for software updates

### Battery Drains Quickly

- Reduce screen brightness
- Disable always-on display
- Turn off unused sensors (GPS, Wi-Fi)
- Check [Bluetooth settings](settings/bluetooth.md) for unnecessary connections
