# Bluetooth

## Pairing a Device

1. Open **Settings > Network > Bluetooth**
2. Enable Bluetooth and tap **Scan**
3. Select the device you want to pair

## Supported Profiles

- **A2DP** -- Wireless audio streaming
- **HFP** -- Hands-free calls
- **BLE** -- Low energy sensors and accessories

See [Wi-Fi Setup](wifi.md) for network-related settings.
