# Notifications

## Enabling Notifications

1. Open the companion app on your phone
2. Go to **Settings > Notifications**
3. Select which apps can send notifications to your watch

## Managing Notifications

- **Swipe left** to dismiss
- **Tap** to view details
- **Long press** to mute the app

## Do Not Disturb

Enable DND mode to silence all notifications:

- **Settings > Notifications > Do Not Disturb**
- Schedule quiet hours (e.g., 22:00--07:00)
