# Health Check

## Heart Rate Monitor

The watch continuously measures your heart rate using an optical sensor.

![Heart Rate Screen](./images/heart-rate.png)

## Sleep Tracking

Wear the watch while sleeping to get insights on your sleep quality:

- **Deep sleep** duration
- **Light sleep** duration
- **REM sleep** cycles
- **Sleep score** (0--100)

## Activity Tracking

Daily metrics tracked automatically:

| Metric | Goal |
|--------|------|
| Steps | 10,000 |
| Calories | 500 kcal |
| Distance | 5 km |
