# Notifications

## Enabling Notifications

1. Open the companion app on your phone
2. Go to **Settings > Notifications**
3. Select which apps can send notifications to your watch

## Managing Notifications

- **Swipe left** to dismiss
- **Tap** to view details
- **Long press** to mute the app

## Do Not Disturb

Enable DND mode to silence all notifications:

- **Settings > Notifications > Do Not Disturb**
- Schedule quiet hours (e.g., 22:00--07:00)

# Bluetooth

## Pairing a Device

1. Open **Settings > Network > Bluetooth**
2. Enable Bluetooth and tap **Scan**
3. Select the device you want to pair

## Supported Profiles

- **A2DP** -- Wireless audio streaming
- **HFP** -- Hands-free calls
- **BLE** -- Low energy sensors and accessories

See [Wi-Fi Setup](settings/wifi.md) for network-related settings.

# Wi-Fi Setup

## Connecting to Wi-Fi

1. Open **Settings > Network > Wi-Fi**
2. Select your network from the list
3. Enter the password and tap **Connect**

## Troubleshooting Wi-Fi

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| Network not found | Move closer to the router |
| Connection drops | Restart the watch |
| Slow speed | Check if other devices are connected |

# Troubleshooting

## Common Issues

### Watch Won't Turn On

1. Charge the watch for at least 30 minutes
2. Press and hold the side button for 10 seconds
3. If the problem persists, contact support

### Screen Is Unresponsive

1. Force restart: hold both buttons for 15 seconds
2. Check for software updates

### Battery Drains Quickly

- Reduce screen brightness
- Disable always-on display
- Turn off unused sensors (GPS, Wi-Fi)
- Check [Bluetooth settings](settings/bluetooth.md) for unnecessary connections
